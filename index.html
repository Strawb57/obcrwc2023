Welcome to the OBC RWC2023 total points competition

Introduction

Welcome to the Old Barn Club (OBC) Rugby World Cup (RWC) 2023 total points competition! This is your chance to test your knowledge to guess the total number of points scored by all the teams over the total of 48 games. There will be 4 prizes for top 4 entries closes to the total. So what are you waiting for make a guess for the total points!

The RWC has now been running every four years since 1987. This year it is being held in France with the Final being played at the Stade de France in Paris. The format of the tournament this year consists of 4 pools with the following teams:

  • Pool A
  • France Italy Namibia New Zealand Uruguay
  • Pool B
  • Scotland Ireland, South Africa Romania Tonga
  • Pool C
  • Wales Australia Fiji Georgia Portugal
  • Pool D
  • England Argentina Japan Samoa Chile

After the Pool stage there will be quarter finals, semi finals, 3rd/4th place playoff and final. Sports_Rugby

Rules

1, Nearest the total wins!

2, There are 4 prizes. £125 for 1st prize, £65 for 2nd prize, £40 for 3rd prize and £20 for 4th prize.

3, If two entries are closes to a prize then then next prize is incorporated into the prize. i.e. if 2nd place is shared between 2 entries then £105 will be split between the entrants. If the 4th prize is shared the 2 entries will split the £20.Sports_Rugby

About

This website is under construction and will hopefully support the OBC RWC 2023 total points competition. In the past similar competitions have been run for the Spring 6 Nations.

The website should be updated every evening after games have been played. This will allow the entrants to view the results and statistics more frequently than waiting to after the weekend to view the results and statistics on the OBC Sports bar notice board.

The website originally included much more functionality but has now been simplified to just support the results/statistics and information links.

If you have any new requirements for the website please speak to Aubrey. If they provide value and time is available they will be included.Sports_Rugby
