Introducton

Welcome to the Old Barn Club (OBC) Rugby World Cup (RWC) 2023 total points competition! This is your chance to test your knowledge to guess the total number of points scored by all the teams over the total of 48 games. There will be 4 prizes for top 4 entries closes to the total. So what are you waiting for make a guess for the total points!"

The RWC has now been running every four years since 1987. This year it is being held in France with the Final being played at the Stade de France in Paris. The format of the tournament this year consists of 4 pools with the following teams:

  • Pool A
  • France Italy Namibia New Zealand Uruguay
  • Pool B
  • Scotland Ireland, South Africa Romania Tonga
  • Pool C
  • Wales Australia Fiji Georgia Portugal
  • Pool D
  • England Argentina Japan Samoa Chile

After the Pool stage there will be quarter finals, semi finals, 3rd/4th place playoff and final.

Rules

The following rules have been used before for the Rugby Union 6 Nations.

  1. Nearest the total wins
  2. 4 prizes
  3. If two entries are closes to the result then the next prize is summed to the prize and then the money shared. The next prize is lost.

About

This website app was constructed to support the OBC RWC 2023 total points competition. In the past similar competitions have been run for the Spring 6 Nations. The 6 Nations total points competitions have been run just using the OBC sports bar notice board but the OBC RWC 2023 total points competition will use this app to support the notice board.
